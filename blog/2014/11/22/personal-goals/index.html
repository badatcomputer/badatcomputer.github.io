Personal Goals

I feel like it is incredibly important to write down goals for the next year before the conclusion of the current year. It helps me navigate the unstructured holiday time to know that I can start imposing structure once the season is over. I also feel like it important to make those goals as specific as possible and time bound them to reasonable, but agressive dates.

I have had some challenges over the last year that have put me further back than I would have liked. I am not terribly upset by this because I want fitness and specifically strength to be a life long pursuit. There will more than occasionally be times that I have setbacks and will still need to continue. I have rebuilt most of my strength, but am by no means my strongest.

I plan on using 6 datapoints to monitor my strength progress. I choose the 4 traditional powerlifting lifts and 2 olympic movements to be these indicators

I also hope to get a couple datapoint on energy system work. Right now I think this will be 5k time an undetermined measure on the bike.

Current Lifts

Bench - 275 Press - 155 Squat - 455 DeadLift - 525

Clean and Jerk - 275 Snatch - 225

Stated Goals

Bench - 315 (Q2 2015) - 335 (Q4 2015) Press - 175 (Q2 2015) - 185 (Q4 2015) Squat - 475 (Q2 2015) - 500 (Q4 2015) DeadLift - 555 (Q2 2015) - 585 (Q4 2015)

Clean and Jerk - 295 (Q2 2015) - 305 (Q4 2015) Snatch - 245 (Q2 2015) - 255 (Q4 2015)

As Stated the goals for Q2 will put me back at the strongest I have ever been, the later goals for the end of the year will put me on my way for my 3 year goal of totaling over 1500 in a powerlifting competition. Pressing has always been my weakest lifts and will require some additional work to get where I would like it.
